StreakMate

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information StreakMate collects, how we use it, and your rights around it.

1. What We Collect

When you use StreakMate, we collect the following:

  • Apple ID (provided by Sign in with Apple) — used only to identify your account
  • Display name — the name you choose within the app
  • Check-in photos — photos you optionally submit as proof of completing a habit
  • Streak data — habit names, check-in dates, streak state, and partner relationships
  • Push notification token — to send you reminders and partner activity alerts
  • Timezone — to calculate midnight correctly for your location

2. What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your real name or email address
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or analytics services
  • We do not sell your data to anyone

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data only to run the app:

  • To identify your account and match you with your streak partner
  • To display check-in photos to your streak partner only
  • To send push notifications about streak activity (partner check-ins, freeze warnings, reminders)
  • To calculate streak state and history

4. Who Can See Your Data

Your check-in photos and activity are visible only to your streak partner — the person you have an active or frozen streak with. No other users can see your data. We do not make any user data publicly accessible.

5. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, hosted on servers in the United States. Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. Check-in photos are stored in private, access-controlled storage.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Dissolved streaks and their check-in history are retained so you can view your history within the app. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at streakmate-support@outlook.com.

8. Children

StreakMate is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via a notice in the app. The updated policy will always be available at streakmate.app/privacy.

10. Contact

Questions or requests: streakmate-support@outlook.com
